Powering the India

Custom-built LT/HT switchboards, bus ducts, power and motor control centers, PLC and drive panels, and various other related products are the primary focus of Suraj Electricals, a company established in 2017 for this purpose.

The company's manufacturing and painting units are situated in a modern production facility at Shed No -1, Yadav Market, Pataudi Road, near Reliance Fresh, GURUGRAM, HARYANA. The facility is built on a vast expanse of over 30,000 sq. ft., providing ample space for all the in-house manufacturing and painting operations.

Suraj Electricals has a highly skilled team of engineers, supervisors, and support staff who provide invaluable support to the company. Additionally, the company is equipped with an advanced Design Office that enables it to meet the specific requirements of different consultants with ease. With these resources at its disposal, Suraj Electricals is able to deliver exceptional services and products to its clients.

Delivering exceptional products, maintaining top-notch quality, and providing reliable after-sales service, Suraj Electricals has succeeded in earning a vast and contented customer base across diverse industries. The company is committed to achieving excellence in every facet of its operations, ensuring that its clients receive the highest level of satisfaction.

Mission

Our aim is to establish ourselves as a prominent global organization that competes with the best in the industry. We strive to offer our customers superior value by consistently enhancing our products and services, and delivering them at highly competitive prices while ensuring on-time deliveries. Our ultimate goal is to be recognized globally for our quality and excellence.

Vision

Our ultimate goal is to establish ourselves as a leader in the industry and earn the recognition of our customers as a trusted and reliable partner.
